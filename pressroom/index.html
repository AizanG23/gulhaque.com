CEO Aizan Gul Announces Groundbreaking Implementation of Project Swarm in Clark County, Nevada

This strategic deployment marks a significant milestone in establishing advanced infrastructure within the region.

Project Swarm is designed to enhance energy independence and bolster national security through its highly intelligent, self-organizing systems. The initial construction will focus on establishing foundational infrastructure critical for the deployment and robust operation of these advanced systems, ensuring unparalleled precision and adaptive intelligence.
